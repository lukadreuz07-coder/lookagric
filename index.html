Who We Are

Rooted in Nigeria,
Reaching the World

LOOK AGRIC ALLIED LIMITED is a Nigerian farm produce export company headquartered in Ibadan, Oyo State. We specialise in the sourcing, processing, and export of premium agricultural commodities — connecting Nigeria’s richest farmlands to global buyers across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and the Americas.

We work directly with trusted farmers across Nigeria’s most productive agric belts, ensuring every lot meets international standards for quality, purity, and consistency — every shipment, every time.
